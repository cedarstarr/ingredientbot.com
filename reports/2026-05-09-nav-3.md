## Phase 4 вЂ” Recommendation

**Option B remains correct.** No regressions detected. Run 2's reasoning still holds:

1. `/dashboard` (usage stats) is semantically distinct from settings/billing вЂ” promoting it to its own INSIGHTS section is honest IA.
2. `/import` belongs with content-acquisition (RECIPES) because it produces a recipe; placing it under KITCHEN miscategorizes it as a cooking action.
3. "Recently Viewed" is more specific than "History" for the recipe-browsing context.
4. In-app Privacy/Terms footer fixes the previous gap where authenticated users could only reach legal pages by signing out.
5. Option A's "Cook Now" mixes a destination (Kitchen) with a content step (Import). Option C reorganizes more chrome than the IA problem warrants.

**SEO note:** All 13 app routes are auth-gated and noindex by middleware; nav structure has zero organic-search impact. Public surface (`/`, `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/r/[slug]`) has its own dedicated footer nav, unchanged.
