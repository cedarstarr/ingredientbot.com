# Ingredient Bot — Site Research Raw Lists
**Date**: 2026-04-09
**Niche**: AI pantry-to-recipe generator — enter what ingredients you have, get streaming recipe suggestions

## List A — Top 5 Mainstream/Legacy Competitors: 50 Features

### A1. SuperCook (supercook.com)
**Tier**: Mainstream/Established | **Pricing**: Free | **Core**: Database-matching engine with 11M+ recipes from 18,000 sites

1. Ingredient list input — type what you have, see recipes you can make right now
2. "Missing one ingredient" recipe suggestions — shows what you're almost able to make
3. Pantry state persistence — saves your ingredient list between sessions
4. Dietary filters (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)
5. Voice dictation mode for adding ingredients
6. Multi-language support (20 languages)
7. Cuisine category filtering
8. Recipe bookmarking
9. Aggregated recipes from external sites (no original content)
10. Mobile app (iOS + Android)
**User complaints**: Tells you you have ingredients you didn't add; can't filter by cooking equipment (no crockpot filter); lag in app performance; no AI generation — just database matching; results feel stale; overwhelming number of results with no quality ranking

### A2. DishGen (dishgen.com)
**Tier**: Mainstream/Growing | **Pricing**: Free (limited credits) / Premium $72/yr / Pro $159/yr | **Core**: Prompt-to-recipe AI generation

11. Free-text prompt input ("make something Italian with chicken and tomatoes")
12. Weekly recipe credit system — gamified usage limits
13. AI meal planner — full week plans tailored to goals
14. Meal plan editing — swap individual meals without regenerating the whole plan
15. Personalized AI profile that learns preferences over time
16. Goal-based meal planning (weight loss, muscle gain, budget)
17. AI-generated recipe images (Pro tier)
18. Website widget integration for businesses
19. Save, bookmark, and share recipes
20. Earn credits via uploads, comments, or social shares
**User complaints**: Credits run out quickly on free tier; AI generates recipes that can have unusual ingredient combinations; no streaming — you wait for full generation; no split-panel UX; image generation is slow; Pro tier feels expensive for what you get

### A3. ChefGPT (chefgpt.xyz)
**Tier**: Mainstream/Growing | **Pricing**: Free (10 generations/mo) / Pro ~$2.99/mo | **Core**: Multi-mode AI cooking assistant

21. PantryChef mode — input available ingredients, get tailored recipes
22. MasterChef mode — generate recipes from cuisine preferences
23. MacrosChef mode — generate recipes hitting specific macro targets (protein/carbs/fat)
24. MealPlanChef mode — week-long meal plan generation
25. PairPerfect mode — wine and beer pairing suggestions
26. MixologyMaestro — cocktail/mocktail recipe generation
27. AI calorie tracking via photo logging
28. Pantry management (real-time ingredient inventory)
29. AmazonFresh + Instacart grocery integration
30. Mobile-only app (web app deprecated)
**User complaints**: Web app deprecated (mobile-only); free tier severely limited (10/mo); calorie tracking mobile-only; 3-day trial then paywall; interface feels cluttered with too many "Chef modes"; no streaming; no split-panel design

### A4. Cooklist (cooklist app)
**Tier**: Mainstream/Established | **Pricing**: Free / $3.99/wk / $6.99/mo / $39.99/yr | **Core**: Pantry-tracking + loyalty card sync

31. Grocery loyalty card integration — auto-imports purchase history
32. Barcode scanner for pantry items
33. Receipt scanning to update pantry
34. 1M+ product catalog for ingredient identification
35. Real-time price comparison across grocery stores
36. Expiration date tracking
37. Smart shopping list auto-categorized by aisle
38. Meal planning with dietary filters
39. Pantry-to-recipe matching (uses what you actually bought)
40. Cross-device sync
**User complaints**: Only works with US grocery loyalty programs; requires giving access to purchase history (privacy concern); complex setup; not an AI generation tool — just recipe search by pantry state; premium pricing steep for a pantry tracker

### A5. Mealime
**Tier**: Mainstream/Established | **Pricing**: Free / Pro ~$5.99/mo | **Core**: Structured meal planning for households

41. Weekly meal plan generation based on dietary preferences
42. Shopping list auto-generated from meal plan
43. Grocery delivery integration
44. Serving size scaling
45. Allergen filtering (nuts, dairy, gluten, etc.)
46. Family plan support (multiple eaters, preferences)
47. Step-by-step recipe instructions with timers
48. Clean, minimalist recipe cards
49. Recipe import from URL
50. Nutritional info per recipe and per week totals

## List C — Pure AI First-Principles List: 50 Features

For: AI pantry-to-recipe generator — enter what ingredients you have, get streaming recipe suggestions

1. **Streaming recipe generation** — real-time token-by-token output so users see the recipe forming, not a loading spinner
2. **Split-panel kitchen UI** — left: ingredient input; right: streaming recipe; feels like a conversation with a chef
3. **Multi-ingredient free-text input** — "I have chicken, garlic, olive oil, and some wilted spinach" natural language parsing
4. **Recipe modification toolbar** — one-click: make it vegan, double servings, simplify steps, spice it up, make it faster
5. **Ingredient substitution suggestions** — "don't have X? Use Y instead" proactively offered during generation
6. **Photo-based ingredient detection** — snap your fridge/counter, AI identifies ingredients from image
7. **Saved recipe collection** — personal cookbook of all generated recipes
8. **Recipe detail page** — structured view with ingredients list, numbered steps, time estimates, difficulty level
9. **Nutrition estimate per recipe** — calories, protein, carbs, fat calculated automatically
10. **Pantry state persistence** — "your pantry" saved between sessions so you don't re-enter every day
11. **Meal planner** — drag saved recipes onto a weekly grid (Mon–Sun, breakfast/lunch/dinner)
12. **Grocery list generation** — from meal plan, identify missing ingredients vs pantry state
13. **Dietary profile** — store user preferences (vegan, nut allergy, low-sodium) applied to every generation
14. **Recipe history** — every recipe you've generated, searchable, with timestamps
15. **Freemium gate** — N free recipes per month, Pro for unlimited
16. **Recipe regeneration** — one click to get a completely different recipe with the same ingredients
17. **Cooking mode** — full-screen step-by-step view with tap-to-advance, keeps screen on
18. **Serving size slider** — adjust servings 1–12 and all quantities scale
19. **Prep time filter** — "I have 20 minutes" constrains generation to quick recipes
20. **Cuisine selector** — "make it Thai / Italian / Mexican" prefix modifier
21. **Difficulty level selector** — beginner / intermediate / advanced
22. **Recipe sharing** — public permalink for each saved recipe, shareable link
23. **Leftover mode** — input "leftovers from last night + these pantry items" optimization
24. **Expiry-first mode** — flag ingredients expiring soon, AI prioritizes using them
25. **Print recipe** — clean print-friendly recipe view
26. **Recipe import** — paste a URL, AI imports and saves to your cookbook
27. **Recipe tagging** — tag recipes manually or auto-tag by cuisine, method, protein type
28. **Recipe search** — full-text search across all saved/generated recipes
29. **Nutritional summary over time** — weekly/monthly nutrition roll-up from meal planner
30. **Ingredient usage history** — "you've cooked with chicken 12 times this month"
31. **Budget mode** — prefer cheaper ingredient combinations
32. **Family profile** — multiple eaters with different restrictions, generate one meal that satisfies all
33. **2FA / account security** — TOTP 2FA on user accounts
34. **Admin panel** — user management, generation logs, AI prompt inspection
35. **Audit logging** — track security-relevant events
36. **Rate limiting on AI endpoints** — prevent abuse of expensive AI calls
37. **Recipe collections** — organize saved recipes into named folders/boards
38. **Recipe card PDF export** — download a formatted recipe card
39. **Weekly meal plan email** — Monday morning digest of the week's planned meals
40. **Smart appliance mode** — "I only have an air fryer" constrains generation method
41. **Recipe completion tracking** — mark recipes as cooked, builds usage history
42. **Season/availability hints** — AI flags if ingredients are out-of-season
43. **Ingredient cost estimate** — rough cost-per-serving estimate
44. **Multi-language recipe generation** — generate in French, Spanish, Japanese
45. **Voice input for ingredients** — speak your pantry contents
46. **Dark mode** — theme toggle
47. **Progressive web app (PWA)** — install on mobile home screen, works offline for saved recipes
48. **Recipe rating** — 1–5 stars on saved recipes, track your favorites
49. **"What's missing?" analysis** — show how many more ingredients would unlock 50 more recipes
50. **Referral program** — share link, both users get extra recipe credits
