## Phase 2: Analysis

**Orphaned pages**: 0 (all excluded pages are intentionally orphaned: PWA utility, print layout, public share links, legal pages)

**Dead nav links**: 0 — all 10 app nav links point to valid routes

**Issues found**:
- `/import` was grouped under "Recipes" section but is a recipe input action (semantic mismatch). Import adds recipes to the system — same mental model as the Kitchen where you generate recipes. Belongs with cooking operations.
- Section label "Recipes" is ambiguous — it could mean the section contains recipes, or leads to recipe features. "Library" better describes saved/archived content.

---

## Phase 4: Recommendation

**Applied: Option C**

Rationale:
- Import Recipe belongs with Kitchen because both are "input" operations (generate a recipe via AI, or import one from a URL). A user starting a cooking session would intuitively look in Kitchen for how to get recipes into the system.
- "Library" is semantically tighter than "Recipes" for a section containing saved, collected, and historic recipes — it connotes an archive rather than an action.
- Account section unchanged — Insights/Upgrade/Settings are all account-management actions.
