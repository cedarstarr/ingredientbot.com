## Issues Found and Fixed

### MEDIUM ‚Äî Dead contact email links (wrong domain)
**Files:** `src/app/terms/page.tsx`, `src/app/privacy/page.tsx`, `src/emails/welcome.tsx`

The Terms and Privacy pages listed `hello@robot-food.com` as the contact email, and the welcome email footer linked to `robot-food.com`. The deployed domain is `ingredientbot.com` ‚Äî these were stale references from an earlier internal project name ("Robot Food").

**Fix:** Updated all three files to use `hello@ingredientbot.com` and `ingredientbot.com`.

### LOW ‚Äî Stale closure: `expiryFirstMode` + `expiringIngredients` missing from `generateRecipes` useCallback deps
**File:** `src/components/kitchen/kitchen-panel.tsx` line 332

The `generateRecipes` useCallback reads `expiryFirstMode` and calls `expiringIngredients()` (a memoized callback), but neither was in the dependency array. This meant:
- Toggling expiry-first mode wouldn't cause `generateRecipes` to close over the new value
- The debounce auto-trigger on ingredient change (line 396) would call a stale version that always treated `expiryFirstMode` as `false`

**Fix:** Added `expiringIngredients` and `expiryFirstMode` to the deps array.
